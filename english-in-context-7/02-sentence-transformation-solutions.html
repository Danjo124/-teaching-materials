English in Context 7 | SOLUTIONS
1. "I didn't steal the money," Tom said.
DENIED
Tom denied stealing / having stolen the money.
2. It wasn't necessary for you to buy a present.
HAVE
You didn't have to buy / needn't have bought a present.
3. I'm certain that Sarah didn't take your keys.
HAVE
Sarah can't/couldn't have taken your keys.
4. Perhaps they got lost on the way here.
MAY
They may have got(ten) lost on the way here.
5. I regret not studying harder for the exam.
WISH
I wish I had studied harder for the exam.
6. Someone broke into our house while we were on holiday.
HAD
We had our house broken into while we were on holiday.
7. It's possible that she forgot about the meeting.
MIGHT
She might have forgotten about the meeting.
8. "Why don't we go to the cinema?" Emma suggested.
GOING
Emma suggested going to the cinema.
9. I haven't seen Julia for three months.
LAST
The last time I saw Julia was three months ago.
10. The house was so expensive that we couldn't afford it.
TOO
The house was too expensive for us to afford.
11. Unless you leave now, you'll miss the train.
IF
If you don't leave now, you'll miss the train.
12. They made us wait for over two hours.
WERE
We were made to wait for over two hours.
