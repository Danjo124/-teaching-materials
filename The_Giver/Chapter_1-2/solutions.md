# Answer Key: The Giver - Chapters 1 & 2

## Part 2: Fill in the Blanks

## Part 3: Open Questions
1. Gabriel is not meeting the community's growth standards and is at risk of being "released." Jonas's father brought him home at night to provide extra care and comfort.
2. For major transgressions, the punishment is "release" from the community. At this point in the story, the characters describe it as being sent "Elsewhere," but the true nature remains mysterious and final.
3. The Ceremony of Twelve is when children receive their permanent adult job assignments. Jonas's mother is concerned because once the assignment is given, their lives change completely and they lose some of their childhood connections.
