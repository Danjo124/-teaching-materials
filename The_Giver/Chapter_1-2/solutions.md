# SOLUTIONS (Teacher Copy)

### Section I: Vocabulary
1. **Remorse**: Deep regret or guilt.
2. **Chortle**: A breathy, gleeful laugh.
3. **Gravitate**: To move toward something.
4. **Serene**: Calm, peaceful.
5. **Infraction**: A violation of a rule.

### Section II: Cloze
6. apple
7. bathe
8. Roberto
9. pale
10. care/bathing

### Section III: Analysis
11. Most have dark eyes; pale eyes are rare and linked to the 'Receiver' ability.
12. It changed briefly in mid-air (flicker of color/depth).
13. A place where the elderly live before release; it provides care and respect.
14. A celebration of the person's life, speeches, and then they go to the Releasing Room.
15. Because of his eyes and his experience with the apple.

### Section IV: Discussion
16. Subjective; often seen as eerie by readers but peaceful by the community.
17. To prevent conflict, jealousy, and pain.
18. Personal response.
19. They value modesty and avoid sexual or individualizing distinctions.
