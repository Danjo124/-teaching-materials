# SOLUTIONS (TEACHER COPY)

## Section I: Vocabulary
1. **Remorse**: Deep regret or guilt.
2. **Chortle**: A breathy, gleeful laugh.
3. **Gravitate**: To move toward or be attracted to something.
4. **Serene**: Calm, peaceful, and untroubled.
5. **Infraction**: A violation or infringement of a law or agreement.

## Section II: Analysis
1. The apple "changed" in mid-air (color/depth). Jonas was confused because everything is monochromatic.
2. Pale eyes are rare; they suggest genetic variety exists despite "Sameness."

## Section III: The House of the Old
1. The ritual shows care but total separation from productive community life.
2. Release of a newchild is a tragedy; release of the elderly is a celebration. Both are mysterious.
