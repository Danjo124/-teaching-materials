# Vocabulary Training: Unit 6 - The United States of America

## Part 1: US History and Society
Define the following terms in the context of US politics:
1. **Emancipation:** The freeing of slaves after the Civil War.
2. **Segregation:** The systemic separation of people based on race (Jim Crow era).
3. **Social Mobility:** The ability of individuals to move up in social class.
4. **Foreign Policy:** A government's strategy in dealing with other nations.

## Part 2: Figurative Language & Poetry
Amanda Gorman's speech style involves:
*   **Rhyme & Assonance:** "roll over boulders", "jumble and stumble".
*   **Homophones:** writer / right; actions / reaction.
*   **Rhythm:** repetitive patterns for emphasis.

## Part 3: Social Activism - Gun Control
*   **Current State:** Most citizens can own guns, but laws vary significantly between states (e.g., 'concealed carry' vs 'open carry').
*   **Prohibitions:** People with criminal records or serious mental health issues are usually barred.
*   **Sentiment:** A rising majority supports stricter gun laws in the US.
