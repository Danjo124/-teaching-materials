# Vocabulary Training: Unit 4 - Trends at Work

## Part 1: Modern Employment Terms
Match the term to its definition:

1. **Emerging economy** -> A nation's economy that is progressing toward becoming advanced.
2. **To offshore** -> Moving a business process to another country.
3. **Zero-hour contract** -> An agreement where the employer is not obliged to provide any minimum working hours.
4. **Glass ceiling** -> An unofficial barrier to advancement in a profession, especially affecting women/minorities.
5. **Labour shortage** -> When there are not enough people available to work.
6. **Collective bargaining** -> Negotiation of wages and other conditions of employment by an organized body of employees.

## Part 3: Reading Analysis - "Balanced vs. Merged Lifestyles"
*   **Balanced:** Sees work and private life as separate opposites.
*   **Merged:** Reconciles demands; switches seamlessly between work and life (facilitated by technology).
*   **Impact:** Lines are blurred; can lead to longer, more hectic days without a clear end to the workday.
