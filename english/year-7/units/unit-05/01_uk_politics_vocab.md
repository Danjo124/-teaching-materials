## Part 1: Politics and Relations
Choose the best verb to complete the historical context: *establish, damaged, forge, negotiated, strengthened*.

1. The UK sought to **forge** new alliances after leaving the EU.
2. The Good Friday Agreement was **negotiated** in 1998 to end "The Troubles".
3. Brexit has **strengthened** / **damaged** political relations between Scotland and England (depending on perspective).

# Grammar Focus: Adjectives vs. Adverbs
*   The conflict was **bitterly** (adv) painful.
*   The relations remained **fiercely** (adv) resentful.
*   They came **dangerously** (adv) close to a crisis.
