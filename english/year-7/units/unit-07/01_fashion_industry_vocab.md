## Part 3: Who Does What in Fashion?
Define these roles:
*   **Seamstresses:** Sew garments for designers/brands.
*   **Models:** Present clothes on runways or in photos.
*   **Stylists:** Select and combine clothes; advise on trends.
*   **Make-up artists:** Do models' make-up for shows/shoots.
*   **Photographers:** Take pictures of fashion items for advertising.
