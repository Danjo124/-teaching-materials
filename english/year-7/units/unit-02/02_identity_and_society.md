# Identity and Society: Simon vs. the Homo Sapiens Agenda

## Context: Youth Language and LGBTQ+
Unit 2 focuses on identity. We analyze Becky Albertalli's *Simon vs. the Homo Sapiens Agenda* (adapted as the movie *Love, Simon*).

## Style and Register Analysis
Simon's narrative is known for its authentic teenage voice.
*   **Colloquialisms:** "The thing about...", "I mean...", "It's like...", "freaking", "lame".
*   **Sentence Structure:** Short, often elliptic sentences ("But yeah.", "Honestly.").
*   **Adjectives:** *awesome, epic, hilarious, weird, awkward*.

## Listening Preview: Advice to Parents (Track 4)
According to teenagers, what should parents do to improve the relationship?
1. Admit when you are wrong.
2. Praise me once in a while.
3. Don't dismiss my feelings as "moods".
4. Ask my opinion.
5. Just be honest.

## Writing Task: Diary Entry
Imagine you are Simon. Write a diary entry (approx. 180 words) about the fear of "coming out" to your parents and your need for a "safe haven".
