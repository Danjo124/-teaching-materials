# Vocabulary Training: Unit 8 - Globalization in Practice

## Part 1: Global Supply Chain
Key concepts:
1. **Global supply chain:** The worldwide network of production, transport, and distribution.
2. **Consumer behaviour:** How people make purchasing decisions.
3. **Outsourcing:** Contracting work to external companies (often abroad).
4. **Ethical production:** Manufacturing that respects workers' rights and the environment.

## Part 2: Diagram Analysis
When analyzing a diagram or infographic:
*   Identify the **main message** (What story does the data tell?).
*   Look for **trends** (increases, decreases, stable patterns).
*   Note **comparisons** (Which category is largest/smallest?).

## Part 3: Writing Task - Report
Write a **Report** (approx. 200-250 words) for your school's magazine about the working conditions in the global fashion industry.

### Report Structure:
1.  **Introduction:** State the purpose of the report.
2.  **Body:** Present findings (use data from diagrams/texts).
3.  **Conclusion/Recommendations:** Summarize and suggest actions.
