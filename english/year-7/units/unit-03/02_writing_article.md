# Writing Task: Unit 3 - Lifelong Learning

## Task Description
Write an **Article** for your school magazine about the importance of lifelong learning in the 21st century.

## Requirements
*   **Title:** Engaging (e.g., "Use it or Lose it").
*   **Style:** Informal (use contractions like *can't, I'm, you're* and colloquialisms).
*   **Structure:**
    1.  Introduction: Define lifelong learning.
    2.  Body: Why is it necessary today? (Technological changes, career shifts).
    3.  Conclusion: Give advice to fellow students.

---

# Model Answer Analysis (Teacher)
*   **Register Check:** Does the student use informal language correctly?
*   **Content Points:**
    *   Continuous skill acquisition is vital for success.
    *   Self-motivation and dedication are key drivers.
    *   One can learn about any subject thanks to digital resources.
