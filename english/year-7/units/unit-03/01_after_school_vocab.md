## Part 3: Reading Analysis - "Becoming" by Michelle Obama
*   **Settling in:** The summer programme at Princeton helped her feel at ease before the regular term started.
*   **Standing out:** She realized she was part of a minority (female and a person of colour) in a predominantly white, male environment.
*   **Security:** She enjoyed the crime-free environment but noted the extra effort needed for less privileged students to adjust.
