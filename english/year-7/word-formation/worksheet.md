# Word Formation Practice - Grade 7B (B2/C1 Level)

## Part 1: Suffixes and Prefixes
*Complete the sentences by changing the form of the word in brackets.*

1. The (EXPLAIN) __________ provided by the scientist was far too complex for the general public.
2. It is (ADVISE) __________ to book your tickets well in advance during the peak season.
3. Her (COMMIT) __________ to the project was truly inspiring to the rest of the team.
4. The sudden (APPEAR) __________ of the witness changed the entire course of the trial.
5. Many people find the (KIND) __________ of strangers to be very moving.
6. The (GROW) __________ of the technology sector has been exponential over the last decade.
7. We need to find a (SOLVE) __________ to this problem before the deadline.
8. His (BEHAVE) __________ at the party was completely unacceptable.
9. There is a wide (VARY) __________ of opinions on the new government policy.
10. The (STRENGTH) __________ of the bridge was tested before it was opened to traffic.

---

## Part 2: Advanced Word Formation
*Fill in the gaps with the correct form of the root word.*

1. (EMPLOY) __________ is a major issue in many developing countries.
2. The (THREAT) __________ of global warming is one of the greatest challenges of our time.
3. It was a (MEMORY) __________ occasion that we will all cherish forever.
4. The (DESCRIBE) __________ she gave of the suspect was incredibly detailed.
5. (CURIOUS) __________ killed the cat, as the old saying goes.
6. The (MAINTAIN) __________ of the building is the responsibility of the landlord.
7. She made a (REMARK) __________ recovery after the surgery.
8. (CONFLUENCE) __________ of several factors led to the economic crisis.
9. The (ACCURATE) __________ of the results was questioned by several experts.
10. He is known for his (GENEROUS) __________ towards charitable causes.

---

## Part 3: Open Cloze & Formation
*Think of the word which best fits each gap. Use only one word.*

1. The (SUCCESS) __________ of the mission depended on perfect timing.
2. (PREVENT) __________ is better than cure.
3. There has been a significant (IMPROVE) __________ in his work this term.
4. The (DISTANT) __________ between the two cities is roughly 200 kilometers.

---

# Solutions
**Part 1:** 1. explanation, 2. advisable, 3. commitment, 4. appearance, 5. kindness, 6. growth, 7. solution, 8. behaviour/behavior, 9. variation/variety, 10. strength.  
**Part 2:** 1. Unemployment, 2. threat, 3. memorable, 4. description, 5. Curiosity, 6. maintenance, 7. remarkable, 8. Confluence, 9. accuracy, 10. generosity.  
**Part 3:** 1. success, 2. Prevention, 3. improvement, 4. distance.
