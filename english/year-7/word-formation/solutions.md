# Worksheet: Word Formation - Globalisation
## 20 Sentences for Advanced Learners (Gymnasium Level)

**Instructions:** Read the sentences below. Use the word given in capitals at the end of each line to form a word that fits in the gap in the same line.

1. The __________ of global supply chains has led to lower prices for consumers. (INTEGRATE)
2. Many activists argue that __________ trade practices exploit workers in developing countries. (EQUAL)
3. The __________ of multinational corporations is often greater than the GDP of small nations. (POWERFUL)
4. Rapid __________ has changed the landscape of many rural areas in Asia. (INDUSTRIAL)
5. We must consider the __________ impact of shipping goods across the world. (ECOLOGY)
6. Digital __________ has allowed people to work from anywhere on the planet. (CONNECT)
7. Some critics fear the __________ of culture due to the dominance of Western media. (HOMOGENEOUS)
8. The __________ of fair trade products has grown significantly in recent years. (POPULAR)
9. Consumers are becoming more __________ about the origins of their clothing. (KNOWLEDGE)
10. The __________ of resources is a major concern for future generations. (SUSTAIN)
11. Many countries have seen a __________ in extreme poverty due to global trade. (REDUCE)
12. Economic __________ can lead to vulnerability during a global crisis. (DEPEND)
13. The __________ of the internet has revolutionised how we communicate. (EXPAND)
14. Governments must create __________ frameworks to protect local industries. (REGULATE)
15. Globalisation has led to an increase in cultural __________. (DIVERSE)
16. The __________ of labour to other countries is a controversial topic. (OUTSOURCE)
17. We need to find a __________ solution to the problems caused by overconsumption. (GLOBE)
18. Many people believe that globalisation is __________, regardless of its flaws. (AVOID)
19. The __________ of wealth is often uneven in a globalised economy. (DISTRIBUTE)
20. Technological __________ is a key driver of modern global integration. (INNOVATE)

---

# SOLUTIONS

1. integration
2. unequal
3. power
4. industrialisation
5. ecological
6. connectivity
7. homogenisation
8. popularity
9. knowledgeable
10. sustainability
11. reduction
12. interdependence
13. expansion
14. regulatory
15. diversity
16. outsourcing
17. global
18. unavoidable
19. distribution
20. innovation
