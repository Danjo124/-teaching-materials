# Detailed Semester Planning - English in Context 7 (New Edition)

This curriculum is based on the official teacher service manual for the 7th Grade (AHS).

## Semester 1 (Wintersemester)

### Topic 1: Identity and Relationships
*   **Unit 1: On the way to adulthood** (Sept)
    *   **Vocabulary:** Friendships, relationships, growing up.
    *   **Skills:** Discussing plans/hopes for the future; Reading prose fiction (*Normal People* by Sally Rooney).
    *   **Writing:** Blog post.
*   **Unit 2: Finding your identity** (Oct)
    *   **Vocabulary:** Youth language, gender & sexual identity.
    *   **Skills:** Interacting with parents; LGBTQ+ interview; Giving a presentation; Reading prose fiction (*Simon vs. the Homo Sapiens Agenda*).
    *   **Writing:** Creative writing (Diary entry).
*   **Grammar focus:** Phrasal verbs, register, to-infinitive/-ing, future tenses, reported speech.

### Topic 3: English-Speaking World (UK & USA)
*   **Unit 5: The United Kingdom** (Nov)
    *   **Vocabulary:** Political system, Commonwealth, political developments.
    *   **Skills:** Presenting a Commonwealth country; Understanding infographics; Queen Elizabeth II.
    *   **Writing:** Article.
    *   **Grammar:** Adjectives and adverbs.
*   **Unit 6: The United States of America** (Dec)
    *   **Vocabulary:** USA, political activism (BLM, gun control), figurative language.
    *   **Skills:** Analysing a speech (Amanda Gorman); Understanding political activism; Prose fiction (*This is where it ends*).
    *   **Writing:** Blog comment.

## Semester 2 (Sommersemester)

### Topic 4: Globalization and Fashion
*   **Unit 7: The fashion industry** (Jan)
    *   **Vocabulary:** Fashion industry, fast fashion alternatives.
    *   **Skills:** Talking about designers; Analysing an essay.
    *   **Writing:** Essay.
*   **Unit 8: Globalization in practice** (Feb)
    *   **Vocabulary:** Global supply chain, consumer behavior.
    *   **Skills:** Workers in the fashion industry; Analysing diagrams/cartoons.
    *   **Writing:** Report.
    *   **Grammar:** Participles.

### Topic 5: Media and Advertising
*   **Unit 9: Advertising and marketing** (Feb/March)
    *   **Vocabulary:** Advertising techniques, strategies, product placement.
    *   **Skills:** Analysing ad techniques; Understanding product placement.
    *   **Writing:** Creative writing (Producing an advert).
*   **Unit 10: Advertising and marketing on the internet** (April)
    *   **Vocabulary:** Digital advertising, influencers, social media campaigns.
    *   **Skills:** Learning about influencer world; Analysing search results.
    *   **Writing:** Blog comment.
    *   **Grammar:** Participles.

### Topic 6: Global Perspectives (South Africa & India)
*   **Unit 11: South Africa** (May)
    *   **Vocabulary:** Multicultural societies, apartheid history.
    *   **Skills:** Modern multi-ethnic society; Prose fiction (*Born a crime* by Trevor Noah).
    *   **Writing:** Blog post.
    *   **Grammar:** Mixed tenses.
*   **Unit 12: India** (June)
    *   **Vocabulary:** India history/today, film industry, Bengaluru (IT city).
    *   **Skills:** Summarizing factual texts; Antonyms & figurative language.
    *   **Writing:** Essay, Report.
    *   **Grammar:** Linking words.
