# Unit 8: Clothes & Shopping (1st Year)

## Part 1: Vocabulary Match
1. **Changing room** --- A place to try on clothes.
2. **Receipt** --- A piece of paper that proves you paid.
3. **Size** --- Small, Medium or Large.
4. **Bargain** --- Something very cheap and good.

---

## Part 2: Shopping Sentences
*Use the words: tight, outfit, assistant, size.*

1. These jeans are too **tight**; I cannot close them!
2. I love your **outfit**! The colors match perfectly.
3. Excuse me, I am looking for a shop **assistant**.
4. What **size** do you take? Small or Medium?

---

## Part 3: Simple Questions
1. What is your favorite piece of clothing?
2. Do you like shopping in stores or online? Why?
3. What color are your favorite shoes?
4. Is it important to wear cool clothes?

## Part 4: Discussion
5. **New vs. Old:** Is it better to buy new clothes or second-hand clothes? Why is it good for nature?
