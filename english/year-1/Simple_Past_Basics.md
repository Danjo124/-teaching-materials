# Worksheet: Simple Past Basics
**Name:** ____________________  **Date:** __________

## 1. Regular Verbs
Fill in the correct Simple Past form (add -ed).
1. I ________ (watch) a movie yesterday.
2. They ________ (play) football in the park.
3. We ________ (visit) our grandparents last week.
4. She ________ (clean) her room on Monday.
5. He ________ (cook) dinner for us.

## 2. Irregular Verbs
Fill in the correct irregular form.
1. I ________ (go) to the cinema.
2. They ________ (have) a great time.
3. We ________ (see) a big elephant.
4. She ________ (eat) a pizza.
5. He ________ (buy) a new bike.

## 3. Negative Sentences
Use "did not" (didn't) + base form.
1. I ________ (not / watch) TV.
2. They ________ (not / play) tennis.
3. We ________ (not / go) to school.
