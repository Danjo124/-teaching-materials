# Unit 8: Clothes & Shopping - Vocabulary Practice (1st Year)

## Part 1: Vocabulary Match
Match the clothing items and shopping terms with their descriptions.

1. **Changing room** (n.)  A. A piece of paper that proves you paid for something.
2. **Fitting** (adj.)       B. To wear something to see if it is the right size.
3. **Receipt** (n.)         C. A place in a shop where you can try on clothes.
4. **Size** (n.)            D. Small, Medium or Large.
5. **To try on** (v.)       E. To put on clothes to see if they fit.

---

## Part 2: Shopping Sentences (Cloze)
*Complete the sentences with the correct words: affordable, bargain, outfit, tight, size, assistant.*

1. These jeans are too __________; I can't close the button!
2. I found a real __________ at the market; this jacket was only 5 Euros!
3. Excuse me, could you help me? I'm looking for a shop __________ to ask about this shirt.
4. What __________ do you take? Small, medium, or large?
5. I love your __________! The colors of your skirt and blouse match perfectly.
6. The prices in this store are very __________ (cheap) for students.

---

## Part 3: Simple Questions
1. What is your favorite piece of clothing? (e.g., my red hoodie)
2. Do you like shopping in stores or online? Why?
3. What color are your favorite shoes?
4. Is it important to wear fashionable clothes?

## Part 4: Discussion (Simplified)
5. **New vs. Old Clothes:** Many people buy new clothes every week. Is it better to buy new clothes or to use old ones (second-hand)? Why?
