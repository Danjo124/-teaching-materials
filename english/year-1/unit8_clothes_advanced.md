# Unit 8: Clothes & Shopping - Vocabulary Practice

## Part 1: Vocabulary Match
Match the clothing items and shopping terms with their descriptions.

1. **Changing room** (n.)  A. A piece of paper that proves you paid for something.
2. **Fitting** (adj.)       B. To wear something to see if it is the right size.
3. **Receipt** (n.)         C. A place in a shop where you can try on clothes.
4. **Discount** (n.)        D. Clothing that is the correct size and shape for you.
5. **To try on** (v.)       E. A reduction in the usual price of something.

---

## Part 2: Shopping Sentences (Cloze)
*Complete the sentences with the correct words: affordable, bargain, outfit, tight, size, assistant.*

1. These jeans are way too __________; I can't even close the button!
2. I found a real __________ at the flea market; this jacket was only 5 Euros!
3. Excuse me, could you help me? I'm looking for a shop __________ to ask about this shirt.
4. What __________ do you take? Small, medium, or large?
5. I love your __________! The colors of your skirt and blouse match perfectly.
6. The prices in this store are very __________ for students.

---

## Part 3: Questions
1. Describe your favorite outfit. What clothes do you like to wear on weekends?
2. Do you prefer shopping in physical stores or online? Why?
3. What is more important to you: the price of a piece of clothing or its quality?
4. Explain the difference between "fashionable" and "comfortable."

## Part 4: Discussion
5. **Fast Fashion vs. Sustainability:** Many people buy cheap clothes and throw them away quickly. Discuss the environmental impact of "fast fashion" and how we could shop more sustainably.

## Part 4: Discussion
5. Fast fashion causes pollution and waste. Sustainability means buying less, choosing better quality, or buying second-hand.
