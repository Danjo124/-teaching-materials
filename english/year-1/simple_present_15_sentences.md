# Simple Present - 15 Practice Sentences with Signal Words

## Part 1: Fill in the Blanks
*Fill in the gaps with the correct form of the verb in brackets. Pay attention to the signal words!*

1. I **always** (watch) ____________________ TV after school.
2. She **usually** (go) ____________________ to the park on Saturdays.
3. We **often** (play) ____________________ football in the garden.
4. They **never** (eat) ____________________ pizza for breakfast.
5. He **sometimes** (help) ____________________ his mother in the kitchen.
6. **Every day**, my dog (run) ____________________ in the forest.
7. My friends **seldom** (listen) ____________________ to classical music.
8. **On Mondays**, we (have) ____________________ English lessons.
9. You **rarely** (arrive) ____________________ late for school.
10. **In the morning**, she (drink) ____________________ a glass of milk.
11. We **usually** (do) ____________________ our homework together.
12. My brother **often** (forget) ____________________ his books at home.
13. They **sometimes** (go) ____________________ swimming in the lake.
14. **Once a week**, I (visit) ____________________ my grandparents.
15. He **never** (buy) ____________________ expensive clothes.
