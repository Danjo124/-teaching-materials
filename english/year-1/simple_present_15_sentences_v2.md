# Simple Present - 15 More Practice Sentences

## Part 1: Fill in the Blanks
*Fill in the gaps with the correct form of the verb in brackets. Watch out for the third person singular (s)!*

1. My sister **usually** (read) ____________________ a book before bed.
2. We **seldom** (drink) ____________________ cola with our lunch.
3. **Every weekend**, Jonas (visit) ____________________ his cousins in the city.
4. You **never** (forget) ____________________ your umbrella when it rains.
5. The bus **often** (arrive) ____________________ late on Mondays.
6. **In the evening**, I **sometimes** (play) ____________________ video games.
7. My parents **always** (drink) ____________________ coffee in the morning.
8. She **rarely** (wear) ____________________ a hat in summer.
9. They **usually** (walk) ____________________ to school together.
10. **Twice a week**, he (go) ____________________ to his karate lesson.
11. We **often** (have) ____________________ pizza for dinner on Fridays.
12. **Every morning**, the sun (rise) ____________________ in the east.
13. My friend **sometimes** (send) ____________________ me funny pictures.
14. You **never** (tidy) ____________________ your room on Saturdays.
15. She **always** (say) ____________________ "hello" to her neighbors.

## Part 2: Negative Sentences
*Change these 3 sentences into the negative form (don't / doesn't).*

1. He always drinks coffee. -> ________________________________________
2. They usually walk to school. -> ____________________________________
3. She rarely wears a hat. -> ________________________________________

# Solutions - 15 More Practice Sentences

1. reads, 2. drink, 3. visits, 4. forget, 5. arrives, 6. play, 7. drink, 8. wears, 9. walk, 10. goes, 11. have, 12. rises, 13. sends, 14. tidy, 15. says.
Negative: 1. He doesn't always drink... 2. They don't usually walk... 3. She doesn't rarely wear (or: She usually doesn't wear).
